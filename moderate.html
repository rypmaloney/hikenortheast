What constitutes an moderate hike?

Moderate hikes are when we really start talking about hiking. Where as easy hikes are quite literally "a walk in the park" a moderate hike deals with some sort of elevation change, and and takes a few hours. It's something you usually don't want to be doing on a whim. However, what separates it from a difficult hike is usually either intensity or duration.
