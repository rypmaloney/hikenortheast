Share your own experience

The purpose of HikeNorthEast is personal. I outline hikes that only I have taken personally and share tips tricks and thoughts about them. This is the exact opposite of comprehensive, but there will be no sterile listing of stats (though there will be some stats).

In the spirit of collaboration: I want you to contribute! A single man can only explore so many places. If you took hike that inspired you, visit the contact page, write a review, and I’ll incorporate it in the website.
