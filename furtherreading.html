About this website

HikeNortheast is kind of niche website, I know. The goal is to serve up personal preferences instead of—perhaps more usefully—technical information. Upon beginning this project, I made one important realization. As an individual, it was unlikely I would be able to compete with the depth and quality of data provided by larger hiking/trekking/camping websites. The best I could do was offer an inferior product. So, instead, I thought I would provide something more personal. This website only reviews hikes that I personally have taken, so the information I provide is subjective.

Because hike data alone seems to sterile, I thought I would infuse it with something softer, brighter, and in some ways more meaningful: literature. If this website highlights the outdoor experiences that have influenced me, it ought point to the writing about those types of experiences that does the same. With that in mind, I want to capture the broadest possible audience, so please, contribute! Visit the contact page and send information about a hike or relevant literature that inspires you!
